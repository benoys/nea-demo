📋 Environmental Management (Construction Noise) Order 2022 ✓ 2022 Rev Ed · Current
Source: Policy Repository · chunk_id: POL-CNO-2022-003 · clearance: PUBLIC · Relevance: 96%
Part II — Permitted Hours of Construction Work

§4. General Permitted Hours
Construction work may be carried out only during the following hours:
(a) Monday to Friday: 0730 – 1800 hours;
(b) Saturday: 0730 – 1400 hours;
(c) Sunday and public holidays: No works permitted.
§5. Night Work Permits (NWP)
A contractor may apply to the Agency for a Night Work Permit to carry out works beyond the permitted hours in §4. Works without a valid NWP beyond permitted hours constitute a violation under EPMA Cap 94A §26.

§8. Residential Buffer Zone
Sites within 200 metres of a residential building are subject to enhanced conditions. A noise monitoring device must be installed and readings submitted weekly to the Agency.
